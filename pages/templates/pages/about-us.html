Our Mission

At MiCenterNYC, we are dedicated to helping migrants assimilate into New York City life by providing them with the information they need to obtain necessities.

MiCoreValues

Empathy

We understand that every migrant's journey is unique, and we are committed to approaching each story with an open mind and without judgment. By recognizing their struggles and honoring their resilience, we aim to offer the necessary resources.

Inclusivity

Our unwavering commitment to inclusivity ensures that all migrants, regardless of their homeland or cultural background, will have easy access to our website's resources. Our user-friendly shelter page is designed to help migrants quickly find nearby shelters and provides essential details about each location.

Collaboration

We believe that collaborating and building strong relationships with local shelters is critical to supporting migrants on their journeys. Through collaboration efforts, we can ensure that every migrant is accounted for and given the proper resources.
